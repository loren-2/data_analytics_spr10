# 📊 AI Job Market Salary Analysis (2024–2025)

This project analyzes global salary trends in the Artificial Intelligence (AI) job market using a dataset of 15,000 job postings from 20 countries. It explores how salaries vary across experience levels, company sizes, job categories, education levels, and geographic regions. The analysis also includes temporal trends, skill demand, and correlations between key variables.

---

## 📁 Project Overview

This study was developed using Python (Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn) and Power BI.  
The dataset used is the **AI Job Market Analysis Dataset 2025**, retrieved from Kaggle.
The project aims to provide a comprehensive view of the current state of the AI labor market and the factors that most influence salary levels.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Explore the structure and dynamics of the global AI job market.
- Analyze salary variations across AI disciplines.
- Understand the impact of experience level, company size, and education on salary.
- Identify the most in-demand skills in the AI sector.
- Examine geographic salary differences.
- Study temporal trends in job postings and salary evolution.

---

## 📚 Data Description

The dataset contains **15,000 job postings** with the following characteristics:

- **20 countries represented**
- **Salaries normalized to USD**
- **Experience levels:** Entry, Mid, Senior, Executive  
- **Company sizes:** Small (<50), Medium (50–250), Large (>250)
- **Remote work ratio:** 0 (onsite), 50 (hybrid), 100 (fully remote)
- **Top 5 required skills per job**
- **Education requirements**
- **Industry sector**
- **Time series data from Jan 2024 to Apr 2025**
